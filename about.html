What We’re All About

Aerophantom started out recently in 2022 with just a few people coming together to do what they love. Little did they know that it would grow into the Aeromodelling world. Our dedicated members enjoy meeting often for different seminars and orientations. Passion and enthusiasm go hand-in-hand with everything we do.

Our door is always open to new members, no matter their experience level. We are proud to bring together people from all over CITY and beyond. Come join us at our next Seminar — we’d love to share all that Aerophantom has to offer.

Vision & Values

  • Spread knowledge about aeromodelling in high schools.
  • Providing best platform to every single person so that no one suffer from lack of knowledge
