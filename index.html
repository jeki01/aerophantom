Aeromodelling

Defination of Aeromodelling

The making and flying of model planes. Relating to the making and flying of model planes.Aeromodelling is the activity involving design, development and flying of small air vehicles
It is a very exciting and interesting way to learn, apply and understand science and engineering principles.

Aeromodelling Aim

The Aeromodeling is a creative course , which has as main objective the design, building and flying of model airplanes or helicopters.These models can be scale copies of real airplanes or helicopters, or they are specially constructed aircraft models to take advantage of some flying characteristics like speed or duration of flight.

Types of Aeromodelling

Fighter aircraft models.
Transport aircraft models.
Helicopter models.
Gliders

Advantages of Aeromodelling

To build up the model of actual air crafts which are unable to carry persons and used for sports purpose is called aero-modelling.The models which are flying with the help of engines and propellers are called powered models.
Flying models are usually what is meant by the term aeromodelling.

What We do

LEARN

You study the fundamentals of aeromodelling, including how drones and model airplanes are made and operate. You also learn about many innovative UAV design kinds.

made

Aeromodelling is essentially founded on three principles. Designing comes first, followed by manufacturing and flying, so you can learn all three here. You gain knowledge of designing, manufacturing, and assembling of RC aircraft, hovercrafts, and drones.

fly

The greatest part of aeromodelling, for which we are all working, in my opinion, is flying our own created UAVs. As a result, you can also learn how to fly RC planes, drones, and other UAVs.

Our Mission

The use of drones and other unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) is extremely popular in modern society because they make life easier. However, there are few resources available that can give you the best knowledge and training, and aeromodelling is a very expensive hobby, so many people who might have been interested in learning this technology have given up. In order to give you the greatest information and training regarding aeromodelling, we are here.
